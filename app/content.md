How I made 24-Hour Heartbreak Cards
========

What is this?
--------

I started making my own collectible cards in 2022. Growing up with Pokemon Cards, Magic: the Gathering and Yu-Gi-Oh!, it was always my dream to make my own card game. Although what I imagined in my childhood was full of dragons and spells, as an artist, I decided to make autobiographical cards, i.e., stories about my life.
